Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, Bread Hands Bakery is a celebration of craft, community, and the simple joy of great bread. Founded on the belief that baking is as much an art as it is a science, we hand-shape every loaf with care, bringing together time-honored techniques and bold, modern flavors.

At Bread Hands, we specialize in naturally leavened sourdough, rustic country loaves, and a rotating selection of seasonal pastries—all made with locally sourced, organic ingredients. Whether you're here for a classic baguette, a hearty multigrain loaf, or a flaky croissant, every bite tells a story of passion and patience.

More than just a bakery, Bread Hands is a gathering place. We host weekly bread-making workshops, cozy morning coffee hours, and occasional pop-up dinners featuring local chefs. Our space is designed to bring people together over warm bread and good conversation.

Stop by, grab a loaf, and experience the magic of Bread Hands—where every bake is made with heart, soul, and, of course, hands.
